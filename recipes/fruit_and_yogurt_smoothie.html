Fruit and Yogurt Smoothie

Description

This yogurt recipe is delicious! You can substitute the strawberries for any berry or fruit you prefer instead.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup strawberries
  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1/4 cup pineapple juice
  • 1 1/2 teaspons white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon orange juice
  • 1 teaspoon milk

Steps

  1. Combine strawberries, banana, yogurt, pineapple juice, sugar, orange juice, and milk into a blender; blend until smooth. It's that easy!
